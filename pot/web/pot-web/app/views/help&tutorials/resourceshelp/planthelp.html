Help : Plant and Equipment - Register

PLANT AND EQUIPMENT REGISTER : Plant register in Resources holds all the Plant/Equipment details of complete Project (in All EPS ID’s). At the time of registering the plant, it can be assigned to any project in an EPS.
SEARCH : To Search Records based on provided Search Criteria.
RESET : To Reset Page with default page load view.
CREATE : To create new Plant.
Procurement & Delivery Details
Deployment History : In this tab, they can edit/update the details of Plant Services. ex: Mobilization date,…etc.
Charge out rates : In this tab, they can edit/update the details of Plant charge out rates. ex: Rate/Unit[Normal Shift , Double Shift],…etc.
Utilization Records : In this tab, It will show the records automatically if any utilization will happen.
Log Book Records - Log Book : It will show the details of system date to last month details. they can edit/update the details of Log book.
Log Book Records - Plant Working Days : It will show the details of working/Non-working days.
Service History & Repairs - Service History : User can save the service history details on this tab.
Service History & Repairs - Records of repair : User can save the Repair history details on this tab.
Depreciation and Salvage Value : In this tab, they can edit/update the details of Plants Life status and Asset value status.
Request for Transfer : After completion of a plant job in a project, plant/equipment needs to be transferred to another project. By clicking the new request for plant transfer, after selecting a plant row, a notification will be sent to the approver to transfer the plant to its inter project.
Approval for Transfer : Transfer of plant will be done by clicking this button. After requesting for transfer of a plant, approver transfers the plant by clicking transfer button. After transferring plant, the plants Inter Project will become its current project and current project will become his past experience.
