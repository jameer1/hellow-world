Help : Master Employee List

MASTER EMPLOYEE LIST : Employee register in Resources holds all the employee details of complete Project (in All EPS ID’s). At the time of registering the employee, he can be assigned to any project in an EPS.
SEARCH : To Search Records based on provided Search Criteria.
RESET : To Reset Page with default page load view.
CREATE : To create new Employee.
Enrollment/Promotion : In this tab, they can edit/update the details of Employee based on the selection list of Employee’s. ex: Enroll date,…etc.
Employee Service History : In this tab, they can edit/update the details of Employee Services. ex: Mobilization date,…etc.
Charge out Rates : In this tab, they can edit/update the details of Employee charge out rates. ex: Rate/Unit[Normal Time , Idle Time],…etc.
Bank Account Details : In this tab, they can Add the Bank Account details of Employee. ex: Account Number, IFSC Code,…etc.
Medical History : In this tab, they can Create the Medical History details of Employee.
Leave and Attendance : In this tab, they can Add the count of leaves based on Year.
Employee Contact Details : The tab Employee Contact Details holds the contact details of the employee like Address, Phone Number and email.
NOK (Next of Kin) Details : This tab contains the employee's family members or relatives contact details. This is maintained for contacting the employee's family in case of any emergency.
Request for Transfer : After completion of his job in a project, employee needs to be transferred to another project. By clicking the New request for employee transfer, after selecting an employee row, a notification will be sent to the approver to transfer the employee to the inter project.
Approval for Transfer : All the Approval Notification should display on this tab. After transferring employee, the employee's Inter Project will become his current project and current project will become his past experience.
Leave Approval : All the leave request Notification should display on this tab. Approver can do “Approve” or “Reject” the leave request by clicking on “View”.
Leave Request : Employee can apply for Leaves by clicking the New Request for Leave Request.
