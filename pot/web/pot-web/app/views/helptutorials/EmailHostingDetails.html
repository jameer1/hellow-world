Email Hosting Details

If you have the access rights of an administrator, you can perform the following in the Email Hosting Details Section:

  • Create the Email Hosting
  • Edit the Email Hosting details
  • Deactivate an Email Hosting

Create Email Hosting:

This section helps you to Create the Email Hosting.

To Create the Email Hosting, do the following.

  1. Click and the Email Hosting Details window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. On the Email Hosting Details window, enter the following details:
  • Email Host: Enter the companyâ€™s registered Email Host.
  • Email Port: Enter the Email Port.
  • Email: Enter the Email ID.
  • User Name: Enter the User Name.
  • Email Password: Enter the Email Password.
  1. Click to save the Email Hosting Details.

Note: 

  • Emails for all the activities are sent from this registered Email ID.

You have successfully created the Email Hosting.

Edit Email Hosting details:

This section helps you to edit the Email Hosting details.

To edit the Email Hosting details, do the following.

  1. Click and the Email Hosting Details window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. Edit the Email Hosting details.
  2. Clickto save the edited Email Hosting details.

Note:

  • Email Host and Email Port are not editable.

You have successfully edited the Email Hosting details.

Deactivate Email Hosting:

This section helps you to deactivate an Email Hosting.

To deactivate an Email Hosting, do the following.

  1. Select the Host you need to deactivate and click.
  2. A Warning window opens.
  3. Click if you want to deactivate.

You have successfully deactivated the Host. 

Note:

  • On the Warning window, click if you do not want to deactivate the Host.
