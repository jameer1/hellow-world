Leave Approval

You can perform the following in the Leave Approval Section:

  • Search the Leave Approvals
  • View and Approve/Reject the Leave Request

Search Leave Approvals:

This section helps you to search the Leave Approvals. 

To search the Leave Approvals, do the following.

  1. Select From Date and To date.
  2. Select the approval type from the Approval Type drop-down menu.

Note: 

  • User can select from the following, from the Approval Type drop-down menu.
    • Pending For Approval
    • Approved
    • Not Approved
    • All
  1. Click Search available on the right side of the screen.
  2. The search results are shown on the screen as shown in the figure.

Note:  

  • To Date is the current date and From Date is one month before the current date by default.
  • Click Reset to reset the screen to the default view.

You have successfully searched the Leave Approvals.

View and Approve/Reject Leave Request:

This section helps you to View and Approve/Reject the Leave Request.

To View and Approve/Reject the Leave Request, do the following.

  1. Select From Date and To date.
  2. Select Pending For Approval internal status from the Approval Type drop-down menu.
  3. Click Search available on the right side of the screen.
  4. The search results are shown on the screen as shown in the figure.

  1. Click and the Leave Approval window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. View the Leave Approval details and you can perform the following in the Leave Approval window: 
  • Click to Approve the Leave Request.
  • Click to Reject the Leave Request.

Note:  

  • Approve and Reject buttons will be enabled/disabled based on normal time settings given in Projects > Project Settings > Leave Approval.
  • Request for Additional Time button will be shown when Approve and Reject buttons are disabled.

You have successfully viewed and Approved/Rejected the Leave Request.
