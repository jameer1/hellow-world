Scope of Works

SOR, Cost codes are mapped to selected SOE items in this module. Actual Quantities are being updated here according to the usage of SOW item in the Work diary.

You can perform the following in the Scope of Works Section:

  • Search the Scope of Works
  • Edit the Scope of Works

Important:

  • SOW will be auto-filled on creation of SOE, available in Projects>>Projects Library.

Search Scope of Works:

This section helps you to search the scope of works.

To search the scope of works, do the following.

  1. Select the EPS ID/EPS Name or Project ID/Project Name and the Enterprise Projects window opens.
  2. On the Enterprise Projects window, select the project as shown in the figure.

  1. Click Search available on the right side of the screen.
  2. The search results are shown on the screen as shown in the figure.

Note:         

  • Click to expand the SOW structure.
  • Click to collapse the SOW structure.
  • Click Reset to reset the screen to the default view.
  • Click  to view the comments.

You have successfully searched the scope of works.

Edit Scope of Works:

This section helps you to edit the scope of works.

To edit the scope of works, do the following.

  1. Search the scope of works. (See the Search Scope of Works section above)
  2. Click to edit the scope of works.
  3. The Edit Scope of Works window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. To edit the Tangible Item Id, select the Tangible Item Id as shown in the figure.

  1. Click , and then double click the tangible item as shown in the figure.

Note:

  • Tangible Item will be mapped from Enterprise>>Central Library>>Tangible Items Classification.
  1. To edit the SOR Item Id, select the SOR Item Id as shown in the figure.

  1. Click , and then double click the SOR as shown in the figure.

Note:

  • SOR will be mapped from Projects>>Projects Library>>Schedule of Rates.
  1. To edit the Cost Item Id, select the Cost Item Id as shown in the figure.

  1. Click , and then double click the Cost Code as shown in the figure.

Note:

  • Cost Code will be mapped from Projects>>Projects Library>>Cost Code Schedule.
  1. Edit the Start Date, Finish Date and Revised Estimation according to the requirements.
  2. Click to save the edited scope of works.

You have successfully edited and saved the scope of works.
