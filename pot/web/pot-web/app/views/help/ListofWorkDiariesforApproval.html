List of Work Diaries for Approval

You can perform the following in the List of Work Diaries for Approval Section:

  • Search the Work Diaries
  • Approve the Work Diary
  • Client Approval

Search Work Diaries:

This section helps you to Search the Work Diaries.

To Search the Work Diaries, do the following.

  1. Select EPS Name or Project Name, and the Enterprise Projects window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. Select the required projects and click .
  2. Select Login User Records to search the records of the logged-in user (or select All User Records to search the records of all the users).
  3. Select From Date and To date.

Note: 

  • To Date is the current date and From Date is one month before the current date by default.
  1. Click Search and the search results are shown on the screen as shown in the figure.

Note:  

  • The Work Diaries that are submitted for approval are shown in the search results.
  • Click Reset to reset the screen to the default view.

You have successfully searched the Work Diaries.

Approve Work Diary:

This section helps you to Approve the Work Diary.

To Approve the Work Diary, do the following.

  1. Search the Work Diaries (See the Search Work Diaries Section above).
  2. Click and the Work Diary - Internal Approval window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. View the Work Diary details and click .
  2. The Work Diary Approval window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. Enter Comments and click to approve the submitted Work Diary internally.
  • In case of exceeding normal time in Projects > Project Settings > Work Diary tab, click .
  1. The Request For Additional Time window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. On the Request For Additional Time window, select/enter the required details and click to request for additional time and generate a notification.

Client Approval:

To perform client approval, do the following.

  1. Search the Work Diaries (See the Search Work Diaries Section above).

Note:  

  • Submitted For Client Approval is shown on the Work Diary’s Status column.
  1. Click and the Work Diary - Client Approval window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. View the Work Diary details and click .
  2. The Work Diary Client Approval window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. Enter Comments and click to approve the submitted Work Diary.

You have successfully Approved the Work Diary.
