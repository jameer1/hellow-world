Assets Maintenance & Repair Category:

Details required for Assets Maintenance are maintained on this page.

You can perform the following in the Assets Maintenance & Repair Category Section:

  • Create a New Assets Maintenance & Repair Category
  • Edit the Assets Maintenance & Repair Category
  • Search the created Assets Maintenance & Repair Categories
  • Deactivate an Assets Maintenance & Repair Category

Create New Assets Maintenance & Repair Category:

This section helps you to create a new assets maintenance & repair category.

To create a new assets maintenance & repair category, do the following.

  1. Click and the Add Maintenance Category Code window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. On the Add Maintenance Category Code window, enter all the mandatory fields.
  • Click  to create the maintenance category subgroup.
  • Click  to create the maintenance category items.
  • Click to delete the maintenance category subgroup and items.
  1. Click to save the assets maintenance & repair category details.

You have successfully created a new assets maintenance & repair category.

Edit Assets Maintenance & Repair Category:

This section helps you to edit the assets maintenance & repair category.

To edit the assets maintenance & repair category, do the following.

  1. Click to edit the assets maintenance & repair category details.
  2. The Edit Maintenance Category Code window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. Edit the assets maintenance & repair category details.
  2. Click to save the edited assets maintenance & repair category details.

You have successfully edited the assets maintenance & repair category details. 

Search Assets Maintenance & Repair Category:

This section helps you to search the created assets maintenance & repair categories.

To search the created assets maintenance & repair categories, do the following.

  1. Enter the Maintenance Category Code or Maintenance Category Name as shown in the figure.

  1. Click Search available on the right side of the screen.
  2. The search results are shown on the screen.

Note: 

  • Click to expand the assets maintenance & repair category structure.
  • Click to collapse the assets maintenance & repair category structure.
  • Both Maintenance Category Code and Maintenance Category Name can be entered to search the assets maintenance & repair categories.
  • Click Reset to reset the screen to the default view.

You have successfully searched the assets maintenance & repair categories and viewed the search results.

Deactivate Assets Maintenance & Repair Category:

This section helps you to deactivate an assets maintenance & repair category.

To deactivate an assets maintenance & repair category, do the following.

  1. Select the assets maintenance & repair category you need to deactivate and click .
  2. A Warning window opens.
  3. Click if you want to deactivate.
  4. A confirmation Info window opens.

You have successfully deactivated the assets maintenance & repair category.        

Note:

On the Warning window, click if you do not want to deactivate the assets maintenance & repair category.
