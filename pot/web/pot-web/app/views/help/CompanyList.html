Create a New Company in the List:

This section helps you to create a new company on the list.

To create a new company on the list, do the following.

  1. Click and the Add Company window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. On the Add Company window, enter the following details:
  • Company ID: Enter the company ID.
  • Company Name: Enter the company name.
  • Company Category: Select the company category from the dropdown menu.
  • Business Category: Select the business category from the dropdown menu.
  • Business Activity: Enter the business activity under which the company falls into.
  • Company Reg. No.: Enter the company registration number.
  • Company Tax File No.: Enter the company tax file number.
  1. Click to save the company details.

Note: 

  • Click to add multiple company records.
  • Select the company and click to delete the selected company’s record.
  • Use the Create button available on the middle right of the screen, for this section only. For other sections, use the Create button available on the bottom right of the screen.

You have successfully created a new company on the list.

Search the Company Details:

This section helps you to search the created company details.

To search the created company details, do the following.

  1. Enter the Company ID or Company Name and select the status from the Select Status dropdown menu as shown in the figure.

  1. Click Search available on the right side of the screen.
  2. The search results are shown on the screen.

Note:         

  • Both Company ID and Company Name can be entered to search the company details.
  • Click Reset to reset the screen to the default view.

You have successfully searched the company details and viewed the search results.

Activate a Company:

This section helps you to activate a deactivated company.

To activate a company, do the following.

  1. Select In Active from the Select Status dropdown menu.
  2. Click Search available on the right side of the screen.
  3. A list of deactivated companies is shown if any exist.
  4. Select the company you need to activate and click .
  5. A Warning window opens.
  6. Click if you want to activate.
  7. A confirmation Info window opens.

You have successfully activated the company.
