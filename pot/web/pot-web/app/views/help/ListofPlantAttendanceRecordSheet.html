List of Plant Attendance Record Sheets

You can perform the following in the List of Plant Attendance Record Sheets Section:

  • Create a New Plant Attendance Record
    • Copy the Template
    • Request for Additional time
  • Search and Edit the Plant Attendance

Note:         

  • Click or  to navigate through the columns (dates).

Create New Plant Attendance Record:

This section helps you to create a new Plant Attendance Record.

To create a new Plant Attendance Record, do the following.

  1. Click and the Plant Attendance page opens as shown in the figure.

  1. On the Plant Attendance page, select EPS or Project.
  2. Select Month for which you need to enter the plant attendance details, and then click Done.
  3. Select Crew (double-click to select).

Note:

  • Projects > Project Library > Crew List is displayed in Crew.
  1. Click and the Create Plant Attendance window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. On the Create Plant Attendance window, click and the Plant List window opens as shown in the figure.

Note:

  • Enter any plant detail in the Plant Search text field to search the plant(s) and view the search results.
  1. Select the project-related plant(s) and click to add the plant(s) as shown in the figure.

Note:

  • Select the plant(s) and click to delete plant(s).

Copy Template:

To copy the previous attendance template, do the following. 

  1. On the Create Plant Attendance window, click and the Plant Details window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. Select Select Crew (double-click to select), select Month, and then click Search (List of Plants which has attendance for the selected search criteria is shown).
  2. Select the plant(s) and click .
  1. On the Create Plant Attendance window, click and the Plant Attendance is created as shown in the figure.

  1. Select the attendance as shown in the figure.

Note:

  • The full forms of attendance codes are as follows:
    • W - Working
    • NW - Non-Working
    • I - Idle
  1. Click to save the attendance details.

Request for Additional time:

To request for additional time, do the following.

  1. In case of Exceeding normal time in Projects > Project Settings > Plant Attendance tab, select EPS or Project.
  2. Select Month, and then click Done.
  3. Select Crew (double-click to select).
  4. Click Search, and then click .
  5. The Plant Attendance Notification window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. On the Plant Attendance Notification window, select/enter the required details, and click to request for additional time.
  1. Click to save the details.

Note:

  • After saving, a notification is generated requesting additional time.

You have successfully created a new Plant Attendance Record.

Search and Edit Plant Attendance:

This section helps you to Search and Edit the Plant Attendance.

To Search and Edit the Plant Attendance, do the following.

  1. Select EPS Name or Project Name, and the Enterprise Projects window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. Select the required projects and click .
  2. Select Login User Records to search the records of the logged-in user (or select All User Records to search the records of all the users).
  3. Select From Date and To date.
  4. Click Search and the search results are shown on the screen as shown in the figure.

Note:  

  • Click Reset to reset the screen to the default view.
  1. Click and the Plant Attendance window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. Edit the plant attendance details.

To Add Plants and Request for Additional Time, see the Create New Plant Attendance Record Section above.

  1. Click to save the plant attendance details.

You have successfully Searched and Edited the Plant Attendance.
