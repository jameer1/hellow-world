Country and Province Codes

Country details with Financial Year, Financial Half Year, and Financial Quarter Year are maintained on this page. These Country Codes can be used in Finance Centre Master.

You can perform the following in the Country and Province Codes Section:

  • Create a New Country Province Financial Year
  • Edit the Country Province Financial Year
  • Delete a Country Province Financial Year

Create New Country Province Financial Year:

This section helps you to create a new country province financial year.

To create a new country province financial year, do the following.

  1. Click and the Add Country Province Year window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. On the Add Country Province Year window, select the Country Code and Province.
  2. Click available under the Financial year column and the Financial Year window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. On the Financial Year window, select the required from and to dates and click .
  2. On the Add Country Province Year window, click available under the Financial Half year column, and the Financial Half Year window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. On the Financial Half Year window, select the required from and to dates and click .
  2. On the Add Country Province Year window, click available under the Financial Quarter year column, and the Financial Quarter Year window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. On the Financial Quarter Year window, select the required from and to dates and click .
  2. On the Add Country Province Year window, click to save the country province financial year details.

You have successfully created a new country province financial year.

Edit Country Province Financial Year:

This section helps you to edit the country province financial year.

To edit the country province financial year, do the following.

  1. Select the record you need to edit and click .
  2. The Edit Country Province Year window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. On the Edit Country Province Year window, select the Country Code and Province.
  2. Click available under the Financial year column and the Financial Year window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. On the Financial Year window, select the required from and to dates and click .
  2. On the Edit Country Province Year window, click available under the Financial Half year column, and the Financial Half Year window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. On the Financial Half Year window, select the required from and to dates and click .
  2. On the Edit Country Province Year window, click available under the Financial Quarter year column, and the Financial Quarter Year window opens as shown in the figure.

  1. On the Financial Quarter Year window, select the required from and to dates and click .
  2. On the Edit Country Province Year window, click to save the country province financial year details.

You have successfully edited the country province financial year details. 

Note:         

Click to view the financial year details.

Delete Country Province Financial Year:

This section helps you to delete a country province financial year.

To delete a country province financial year, do the following.

  1. Select the country province financial year, you need to delete and click .
  2. A Warning window opens.
  3. Click if you want to delete.
  4. A confirmation Info window opens.

You have successfully deleted the country province financial year.

Note:

On the Warning window, click if you do not want to delete the country province financial year.
